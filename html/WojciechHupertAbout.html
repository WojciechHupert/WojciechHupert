Two Decades of Craft

With twenty years of experience in the design industry, I've seen the landscape shift from print to digital, and now toward AI and spatial computing. My journey has been driven by a constant curiosity about how humans interact with technology.

I've worked with global brands and agile startups, always focused on delivering high-quality, human-centric design that balances aesthetic beauty with functional utility.

Beyond the screen, I produce video, run a podcast, and build real-time environments in Unreal Engine. I believe the best designers are those who never stop making and exploring.
