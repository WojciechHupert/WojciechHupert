Project 01

Lelit Distrikt Experience

Interactive metaverse-style environment with intelligent NPCs. Platform for storytelling, education and entertainment.

The Concept

Lelit Distrikt is a visionary exploration into the future of interactive digital spaces. It combines high-fidelity 3D environments with advanced AI to create a living, breathing world where users can engage in meaningful ways.

Designed as a multi-purpose platform, it serves as a stage for storytelling, an immersive classroom for education, and a vibrant hub for entertainment.

Unreal Engine Environment Render

Intelligent NPCs

The core of the experience lies in its intelligent inhabitants. Powered by custom-integrated AI models, NPCs in Lelit Distrikt possess extensive reasoning capabilities and memory, allowing for dynamic, unscripted interactions.

Users can converse with these characters via text or voice, making the world feel reactive and truly alive.
