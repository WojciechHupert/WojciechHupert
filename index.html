About Me

20 years of design.
A lifetime of curiosity.

I'm a product designer with two decades of experience spanning UX, UI, Graphic Design, and Branding. I work at the intersection of craft, technology, and creative exploration — currently based in Berlin.

Beyond product design, I build real-time environments in Unreal Engine, experiment with AI, design games, produce video, and run a podcast. I believe the best designers never stop making things.
